Germany


Germany is a member of the United Nations and one of the members of the European Union. It is a major power with the world's fourth largest economy by nominal GDP and the fifth largest by purchasing power parity. Moreover, the country has developed a high standard of living and a comprehensive system of social security.

Germany is a country in Western Europe, so it is influenced by a temperate seasonal climate. Therefore the weather may play trick on you in spring. It could be really nice and sunny but it could also rain or snow.
