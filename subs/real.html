Real Work Experience.


A placement provides the prospect to gain hands on work practice that you just can`t get in the classroom. First time job seekers and career changers aren`t usually sought-after candidates, but companies are willing to prepare them as interns and give them the experience they would need to get a job.

It also enables and easier transition from the university environment to the working world. Acquiring experience through internships gives students vital insights on the typical workday in a particular company or field. Internships are like experimental careers to students, and they use this as a tool to diversify their background and as a means to pave the way for a bright future.
