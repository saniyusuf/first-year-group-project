Finding Internships.


NGO's are typically nonprofit and include humanitarian institutes like Amnesty International, health care providers like CARE, research institutes like the Bermuda Biological Station, and various organisations supporting international educational exchange programs such as the Institute of International Education.

Interns are generally in huge demand in these organisaitons locally based offices and sometimes registration can also be done by direct application to overseas offices. Internships with NGO's are often unpaid.There are around 94,000 non-government organisations in over 150 countries. A Handy Tip - An excellent web site for exploring various internship programs and job prospects with NGOs is www.idealist.org.
