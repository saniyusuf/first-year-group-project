Africa.


Cape Town: is the second largest city in South Africa and is the capital of the Western Cape Jurisdiction, as well as being the governmental capital of South Africa, It is located in the south-west corner of the country near the Cape of Good Hope, and is the most southern city in Africa. The city has a population of approximately 3.5 million people. The internships offered at this location are also highly recommended.

Our partners in this region include Connect-123 develops. They administer volunteer, internship and study abroad programs in Cape Town, South Africa. Their semester long Informations Technology Entrepreneurship study abroad program is offered at the University of Cape Town, while internship and volunteer work opportunities are hosted by local companies and non-profit organization.

Almost all of the internship positions available in this region are strictly none paid. However, the companies or organizations responsible for each individual placement are set to supply the basic living commodities and a weekly allowance to all students.
