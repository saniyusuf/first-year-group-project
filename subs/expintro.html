The Feel Of Internship Experience.


As an undergraduate, we have learned so much knowledge from school, but when we graduate from university into the real world, it matters how to transform our knowledge into practical skills? Therefore, so many students choose sandwich placements to stronger their practical ability.

Work Experience: majority of this is always in the second year during university studying. The length of placements varies from two months to one full year. During this period, students need to work with companies or employers with the knowledge which they have learned from there course and put it in practice. Consequently, students can gain the work experience in their study. It is the best way to help them put the theory and practical action together and it will be very helpful to them in the future.

If a student chooses internship, it is not only just making you work, but also you need to learn how to put your theory work knowledge into practical activity. For instance, if you are a student in computer science or information systems, you will always copy the code that the teacher teaches you in class, you often use the code which you are familiar with. However, you need to meet the requirements in the company; it is not enough what you learned from the class. Therefore, you have a driving force that pushes you to learn more. It will also help you to develop the practical knowledge and skills. Besides, you will gain so much benefits not only knowledge. you will need to be equipped with strong psychological quality that the company may be satisfied with, so you will get stressed out and feel more pressure to finish it perfectly.

In other words, you will need to recognize your personal works and commercial works experience. Secondly, learning how to develop team spirit is also an important thing. You need to get along with others and deal with the cases peacefully such as eliminating the differences between the team members. So therefore, if students have internship experience, they will be more competitive in the future job market. They have already had strong practical ability to enter the company.
