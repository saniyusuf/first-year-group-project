Finding Internships.


→Several major international internship programs are sponsored by private enterprisess such as Proctor & Gamble, Coca-Cola, and various others.Though these programs are sometimes restricted specifically for students pursuing MBA.

→ The BUNAC program is very useful for undergraduate students to arrange overseas internships with U.S. or host-country companies. Many undergraduates find that internships with private industry pay enough so as to cover the basic living expenses, but might not include the cost of trans-atlantic airfare.

A Handy Tip - For students having specific companies in mind, should carry out the search in Google by using search terms like "company name + internship" or use websites like www.monster.com, www.careerbuilder.com and many others.
